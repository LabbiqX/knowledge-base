## TL;DR
Anthropic chief scientist Jared Kaplan warns humanity must decide by 2027-2030 whether to allow AI recursive self-improvement. He predicts AI will handle most white-collar jobs in 2-3 years and blue-collar jobs shortly after. While confident aligned AI at human-level is manageable, he fears what happens when AI surpasses human intelligence and begins making even smarter AIs.

---

## Key Points
- Critical decision window: 2027-2030 for recursive AI self-improvement
- AI could do most white-collar work within 2-3 years
- His 6-year-old son will "never outperform AI" on school tasks
- Confident about alignment at human-level intelligence; worried above it
- AI capabilities doubling every 7 months — faster than society can adapt
- Chinese hackers already using Claude for cyberattacks
- Kaplan went from physics researcher to AI billionaire in 7 years
