## TL;DR
Anthropic chief scientist Jared Kaplan warns that by 2027-2030, humanity will face the "ultimate risk" decision of whether to let AI models train themselves. Recursive self-improvement could either create beneficial AGI or allow AI power to snowball beyond control. He predicts AI will handle most white-collar work in 2-3 years.

---

## Key Points
- Kaplan predicts critical AI self-improvement decision between 2027-2030
- Current AI distillation already uses larger models to train smaller ones
- Recursive self-improvement (AI training AI without humans) is the core concern
- AI could handle "most white-collar work" in 2-3 years
- Once AI surpasses human intelligence, "you don't know where you end up"
- Critics note doomsaying can be its own form of hype, distracting from immediate issues
- Yann LeCun and others skeptical LLMs can achieve general intelligence

## 🎯 Anahtar Çıkarım
> Kaplan'ın uyarısı, yapay zekanın kendi kendini eğitme kararının insanlığın en kritik tercihi olacağını vurguluyor. Ancak bu endişeler, AI'nin şimdiki somut sorunlarından (çevre, telif, bağımlılık) dikkat dağıtma riski de taşıyor.
