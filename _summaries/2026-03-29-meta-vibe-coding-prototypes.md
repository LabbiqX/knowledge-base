## TL;DR
Meta product managers are using vibe coding to build prototype apps and show them directly to Zuckerberg and leadership. Joseph Spisak (Meta Superintelligence Labs) says this allows teams to "iterate and explore the space really fast" — turning ideas into working demos in hours or days instead of weeks. Engineers remain essential for production-quality code.

---

## Key Points
- Product managers vibe-coding prototypes and presenting to Zuckerberg directly
- "PMs are actually vibe coding products" — Joseph Spisak, Meta Superintelligence Labs
- Enables rapid iteration: hours/days instead of weeks
- Non-programmers (PMs, designers) can build simple apps independently
- Engineers still needed for safety, stability, and production readiness
- AI starts the process, humans finish the job
- Shifts early-stage ideation from engineer-dependent to team-wide
