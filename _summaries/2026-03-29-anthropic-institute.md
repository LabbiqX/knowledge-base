## TL;DR
Anthropic launched The Anthropic Institute to confront the most significant challenges powerful AI will pose to society. Led by co-founder Jack Clark (new role: Head of Public Benefit), it combines three existing research teams — Frontier Red Team, Societal Impacts, and Economic Research. The Institute will leverage its unique vantage point as a frontier AI builder to inform the public about AI's impacts on jobs, economy, law, and governance.

---

## Key Points
- Combines Frontier Red Team + Societal Impacts + Economic Research under one Institute
- Led by co-founder Jack Clark as Head of Public Benefit
- Key hires: Matt Botvinick (AI and rule of law), Anton Korinek (transformative AI economics), Zoë Hitzig (economics + model training)
- Focus areas: jobs/economy, societal resilience, threat magnification, AI "values", recursive self-improvement governance
- Predicts "far more dramatic progress in the next two years" — AI development is accelerating/compounding
- Two-way engagement: engaging with workers, industries, and communities facing displacement
- Also expanding Public Policy team, opening DC office
- Sarah Heck (ex-Stripe, ex-White House NSC) leads Public Policy
