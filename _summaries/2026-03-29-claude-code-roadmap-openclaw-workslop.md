## TL;DR
Roadmap.sh launched a comprehensive Claude Code roadmap covering vibe coding, agentic loops, MCP servers, plugins, hooks, and subagents. Meanwhile, C-suite executives report no AI productivity boom despite heavy investment. A CEPR study across 12,000 European firms shows AI training is the missing link — organizations that train their people see 4% labor productivity gains with no employment reductions.

---

## Key Points
- Roadmap.sh's Claude Code roadmap helps users go from casual prompting to real agentic workflows
- ML engineer Stephanie Kirmer describes LLMs reshaping daily work but warns of an investment bubble
- Fortune study: C-suite executives haven't seen AI productivity gains despite heavy investment
- CEPR study (12,000 European firms): AI adoption increases productivity 4% on average when combined with training
- Every additional 1% investment in workforce training amplifies AI productivity gains
- Historical pattern: same gap existed with PCs, internet, and mobile adoption

> AI tools are improving objectively, but without training and proper workflows, organizations won't see productivity gains. The CEPR study proves: invest in people, not just tools.
