## TL;DR
Salesforce completely rebuilt Slackbot from a simple notification tool into a full AI agent powered by Anthropic's Claude. The new Slackbot can search enterprise data (Salesforce, Google Drive, calendars, Slack conversations), draft documents, and take actions. Internal testing with 80,000 employees showed 96% satisfaction, 80% continued usage, and employees saving 2-20 hours per week. MrBeast's company reports 90 minutes saved daily per employee.

---

## Key Points
- Complete architectural overhaul: from "tricycle" to "Porsche" (per Parker Harris, Salesforce co-founder)
- Powered by Anthropic's Claude — chosen for FedRAMP compliance; Google Gemini and OpenAI coming next
- Fastest adopted product in Salesforce history: 2/3 of 80K employees tried it, 80% continued
- 96% internal satisfaction — highest for any AI feature shipped by Slack
- Can correlate qualitative and quantitative data (text + uploaded images)
- Creates shared Canvas documents and schedules meetings autonomously
- Available to Business+ and Enterprise+ customers
- Security: only accesses info each user already has permission to view; no customer data used for training
- Competing directly with Microsoft Copilot (Teams) and Google Gemini (Workspace)
- Beast Industries (MrBeast) pilot: 90 minutes saved daily per employee
