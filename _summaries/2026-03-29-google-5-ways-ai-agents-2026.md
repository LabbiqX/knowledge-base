## TL;DR
Google's 2026 AI Agent Trends Report outlines 5 ways agents will reshape business: boosting individual productivity, becoming core to business processes via multi-agent workflows (A2A protocol), enabling concierge-style customer experiences, supercharging security operations, and driving AI-ready workforce training programs.

---

## Key Points
1. **Productivity**: Telus — 57,000 employees saving 40 min per AI interaction; Suzano — 95% query time reduction
2. **Agentic Workflows**: Salesforce + Google building cross-platform agents with A2A protocol
3. **Customer Experience**: Danfoss automating 80% of order decisions, response from 42hrs to near real-time
4. **Security Operations**: Macquarie Bank — 38% more self-service, 40% fewer false positives
5. **AI-Ready Workforce**: Continuous learning plans replacing one-off training
- Shift from routine execution to strategic direction
- 2026 = year AI agents take over most taxing security operations
