## TL;DR
Anthropic expanded its Labs team for incubating experimental products at the frontier of Claude's capabilities. Instagram co-founder Mike Krieger (previously Anthropic's CPO) joins Labs alongside Ben Mann. Ami Vora takes over leading the Product organization. Labs has already produced Claude Code ($1B product in 6 months), MCP (100M monthly downloads), Skills, Claude in Chrome, and Cowork.

---

## Key Points
- Labs team focuses on tinkering at the edge of Claude's capabilities and testing with early users
- Mike Krieger (Instagram co-founder, ex-Anthropic CPO) joins Labs team
- Ami Vora leads main Product organization with CTO Rahul Patil
- Track record: Claude Code (research preview → $1B in 6 months), MCP (100M monthly downloads)
- Recent launches: Skills, Claude in Chrome, Cowork (desktop agentic capabilities)
- Daniela Amodei: "The speed of advancement in AI demands a different approach to how we build"
- Two-track strategy: Labs discovers experimental products, Product scales them responsibly
