## TL;DR
Contextual AI (backed by Bezos Expeditions) launched Agent Composer, a platform for building AI agents in technically demanding industries like aerospace and semiconductors. The key insight: the bottleneck isn't models (now commoditized) — it's context (accessing proprietary docs, specs, institutional knowledge). Early customers report dramatic gains: root-cause analysis from 8 hours to 20 minutes.

---

## Key Points
- "The model is almost commoditized. The bottleneck is context." — CEO Douwe Kiela
- Hybrid architecture: deterministic rules for critical steps + dynamic reasoning for exploration
- Three agent creation modes: pre-built templates, natural language description, visual drag-and-drop
- "One-click agent optimization" with user feedback; sentence-level citations for auditability
- Early results: manufacturer cut root-cause analysis from 8 hours to 20 minutes
- Customers: Qualcomm, Nvidia, Advantest, ShipBob (60x faster issue resolution)
- Pricing starts at $50/month self-serve
- Biggest objection: "we'll build it ourselves" — many teams spend 12-18 months debugging retrieval pipelines
- Highest score on Google's FACTS benchmark for hallucination-resistant results
