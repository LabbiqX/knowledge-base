## TL;DR
Jason Lemkin (SaaStr founder, "Godfather of SaaS") replaced his 10-person sales team with 20 AI agents after two high-paid reps quit. The pivot happened rapidly — from 1 agent in May to 20+ by June. Each agent is trained on the company's best human salesperson's scripts and processes. The empty desks now carry agent names like "Quali" and "Arty." Net productivity is about the same as humans, but agents are more efficient and scale like software.

---

## Key Points
- SaaStr went from 10 human SDRs/AEs to 20 AI agents in one month
- Trigger: two high-paid reps quit; Lemkin said "We're done with hiring humans in sales"
- Agents trained on best human performer's scripts and workflows
- Physical desks labeled with agent names (Quali, Arty, Repli)
- Net productivity similar to humans but more efficient and scalable
- Similar approach used by Vercel: trained sales agent off top performer for 6 weeks
- Security risk: agents need OS-level access, creating more attack surface
- Cost calculus: $150K/year human who will eventually quit vs. loyal AI agent
