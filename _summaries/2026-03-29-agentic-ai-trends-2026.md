## TL;DR
The agentic AI market is projected to grow from $7.8B to $52B by 2030, with Gartner predicting 40% of enterprise apps will embed AI agents by end of 2026. Seven key trends: multi-agent orchestration becoming the "microservices moment," protocol standardization via MCP/A2A, enterprise scaling challenges, governance as competitive differentiator, evolved human-in-the-loop architectures, and more.

---

## Key Points
1. **Multi-Agent Orchestration** — 1,445% surge in inquiries; specialist agents replacing monolithic LLMs
2. **Protocol Standardization** — MCP (tool connectivity) and A2A (agent-to-agent communication) as HTTP-equivalents
3. **Enterprise Scaling Gap** — 2/3 experimenting, fewer than 1/4 in production; workflow redesign > tech excellence
4. **Governance & Security** — Most CISOs concerned but few have mature safeguards; "bounded autonomy" architectures emerging
5. **Human-in-the-Loop Evolution** — Shifting from limitation to strategic architecture; hybrid systems outperform either alone
6. Foundation patterns: ReAct, Reflection, Tool Use, Planning, Multi-Agent Collaboration
7. Small language models viable for many agent tasks
