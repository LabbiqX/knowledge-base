## TL;DR
Nvidia deployed OpenAI's Codex to all 30,000 engineers — one of the largest enterprise AI coding assistant rollouts ever. CEO Jensen Huang's mandate: "every task that is possible to be automated with AI should be automated." Engineers praise the GPT-5.3-codex model for handling complex multi-step workflows without losing context. Nvidia also uses Cursor across engineering teams.

---

## Key Points
- Largest enterprise AI coding tool deployment to date: 30,000 engineers
- GPT-5.3-codex model praised for context management and token efficiency
- Jensen Huang's philosophy: AI automates tasks, not jobs
- Huang cited radiology as proof: more radiologists employed at higher salaries post-AI
- Engineers are also using Cursor alongside Codex
- Despite automation push, Nvidia is hiring thousands more — still ~10,000 short
- New offices opening in Shanghai, Taipei, and across the US

## 🎯 Anahtar Çıkarım
> Nvidia's 30,000-engineer Codex deployment is the strongest enterprise signal yet that AI coding tools have crossed the adoption threshold. Jensen Huang's "automate everything, hire more humans" approach challenges the zero-sum narrative around AI and jobs.
