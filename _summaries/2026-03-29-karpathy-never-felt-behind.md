## TL;DR
Despite coining "vibe coding" and being an OpenAI co-founder, Andrej Karpathy says he's "never felt this much behind as a programmer." He believes he could be 10X more powerful by properly stringing together available tools but calls it a "skill issue." He describes AI coding tools as a "powerful alien tool handed around with no manual" during a "magnitude 9 earthquake" rocking the profession.

---

## Key Points
- Even Karpathy feels behind — "the profession is being dramatically refactored"
- Senses potential for 10X productivity but can't fully capture it yet
- "Failure to claim the boost feels decidedly like skill issue"
- AI coding tools described as "powerful alien tool" with no manual
- Boris Cherny (Claude Code creator) agrees — feels this way "most weeks"
- Cherny: new graduates may adapt better since they don't assume AI's limitations
- "It takes significant mental work to re-adjust to what the model can do every month or two"
- Tools sometimes "shoot pellets" or "misfire" — but occasionally "a powerful beam of laser erupts"
