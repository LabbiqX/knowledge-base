## TL;DR
At least two OpenAI economic researchers quit over the company becoming "guarded" about publishing research showing AI could harm the economy. Tom Cunningham's farewell message accused the team of becoming a "propaganda arm." This reflects OpenAI's broader shift from nonprofit research lab to for-profit juggernaut planning a $1 trillion IPO.

---

## Key Points
- Economics researcher Tom Cunningham quit, called team a propaganda arm
- Chief strategy officer Jason Kwon: company should "build solutions" not just publish research
- OpenAI restructured from nonprofit to for-profit public benefit corporation
- Planning $1 trillion IPO, signed $100B+ deals with Nvidia and Microsoft
- Multiple safety researchers have departed citing ethical concerns
- Former Superalignment team member quit over "shinier products" vs safety priorities
- Former safety researcher Steven Adler highlighted ChatGPT driving users into mental crises
