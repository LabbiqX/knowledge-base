## TL;DR
Anthropic launched Claude Marketplace, letting enterprises apply their existing Anthropic spend toward third-party Claude-powered tools from GitLab, Harvey, Lovable, Replit, Rogo, and Snowflake. This positions Claude as both an intelligence layer and an app platform — countering the narrative that Claude would replace SaaS apps, instead suggesting they're more valuable with Claude integrated.

---

## Key Points
- Enterprises can use existing Anthropic commitment to purchase partner solutions (consolidated invoicing)
- Partners: GitLab (dev), Harvey (legal), Replit (coding), Rogo (finance), Snowflake (data)
- Marketplace is in limited preview; enterprises contact their Anthropic account team
- Raises a question: Claude as direct tool vs. Claude through specialized third-party apps
- Anthropic: "Claude is the intelligence layer. Our partners are the product."
- Similar to OpenAI's ChatGPT App Directory and other AI marketplaces (Salesforce, Lightning AI)
- Challenge: adoption — many enterprises already connect via API, MCP, or have vibe-coded alternatives

## 🎯 Anahtar Çıkarım
> Claude Marketplace signals that Anthropic sees its future not just as a model provider but as an enterprise platform — where specialized partners build the product layer on top of Claude's intelligence, similar to how app stores work for operating systems.
