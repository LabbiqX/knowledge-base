## TL;DR
Benjamin Riley of Cognitive Resonance argues that LLMs will never achieve true intelligence because neuroscience shows human thinking is largely independent of language. fMRI studies prove different brain regions handle language vs. reasoning. People who lose language abilities retain thinking capabilities. A separate study shows AI creativity is mathematically capped at average human level.

---

## Key Points
- Language ≠ intelligence: fMRI shows separate brain regions for language and cognition
- People with aphasia can still solve math, follow instructions, understand emotions
- LLMs emulate communicative function of language, not cognitive process of reasoning
- Yann LeCun agrees: advocates "world models" over LLMs for general intelligence
- Study in Journal of Creative Behavior: AI creativity mathematically capped at average human level
- LLMs are probabilistic systems that can't generate truly novel, non-nonsensical outputs beyond a ceiling
- Meta's Zuckerberg still pouring billions into LLM-based superintelligence despite skepticism
