## TL;DR
freeCodeCamp published a 4-hour course on mastering n8n, an open-source visual workflow automation tool. The course covers building AI agents including Email AI Agent, Research Workflows with OpenAI, Customer Support RAG Agent with Pinecone vector database, Slack agents, multimedia agents (text-to-image/video), and multi-workflow builds with "teams of agents." Also covers Docker and self-hosting with local LLMs via Ollama.

---

## Key Points
- n8n: open-source visual workflow automation connecting apps, APIs, and AI models
- Projects: Email AI Agent, Research Workflow (Perplexity + OpenAI), Customer Support RAG Agent
- Vector database setup with Pinecone for RAG
- Modular Component Patterns (MCPs) for scalable design
- Multi-workflow advanced builds: "team of agents" coordination
- Hosting options: cloud, Docker, self-hosting with Ollama (local LLMs)
- 4-hour course by KodeKloud on freeCodeCamp YouTube
