## TL;DR
Google CEO Sundar Pichai says vibe coding is "making coding so much more enjoyable" and comparable to how blogging/YouTube democratized writing and content creation. AI is writing 30%+ of new code at Google (up from 25%). Meta's product managers are vibe-coding prototype apps for Zuckerberg. Pichai acknowledges risks for large, security-critical codebases but calls it "the worst it'll ever be."

---

## Key Points
- Vibe coding compared to blogging/YouTube democratization of other fields
- Google seeing "sharp increase" in people submitting their first code changes
- AI writing 30%+ of new code at Google (up from 25% in Oct 2024)
- Meta PMs vibe-coding prototype apps to show Zuckerberg
- Non-technical workers (HR, accountants) building their own apps
- Risk acknowledged: not suitable for large codebases needing security
- "It's both amazing to see, and it's the worst it'll ever be"
