## TL;DR
Meta CEO Zuckerberg is establishing a new applied AI engineering organization led by Maher Saba, with an unusually flat structure (50 ICs per manager). The team will build "the data engine that helps models get better, faster." This splits AI work across multiple specialized teams — Wang's Superintelligence Labs, Saba's applied engineering, and Bosworth's technology strategy — building organizational redundancy into Meta's superintelligence push.

---

## Key Points
- New org led by Maher Saba (ex-Reality Labs), reports to CTO Andrew Bosworth
- 50:1 IC-to-manager ratio for speed and reduced bureaucracy
- Focus: data processing, tooling, model evaluations
- Splits AI work across three teams: research (Wang), applied engineering (Saba), strategy (Bosworth)
- Meta Superintelligence Labs already laid off ~600 positions for efficiency
- Projects code-named Avocado and Mango in development
- Philosophy: "elevating individual contributors and flattening teams"
- Organizational redundancy ensures progress even if one team hits friction
